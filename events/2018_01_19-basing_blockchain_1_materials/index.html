Presentation materials of 'The 1st Workshop Basing Blockchain' now available

January 19, 2018

BASE Alliance and the University of Tokyo co-hosted a workshop on ongoing work on academic research on blockchain on January 19th, 2018 at the University of Tokyo.

The materials used at the workshop can be downloadble in the following links.

  • Opening remarks (Japanese) Prof. Kanta Matsuura Professor, Institute of Industrial Science, the University of Tokyo

  • Session 1 / Cyberinsurance and Some Notes on Blockchain Applications (English) Mr. Fenn Gregory Royal Holloway, University of London

  • Session 2 / Presentation 1 : Digital Fabrication and Blockchain (Japanese) Prof. Hiroya Tanaka Professor, Faculty of Environmental Information Studies, Keio University   and Ryosuke Abe Graduate School of Media and Governance, Keio University

  • Session 2 / Presentation 2 : BSafe.Network: International Academic Research Network - Status Update (Japanese) Prof. Shin’Ichiro Matsuo Project Professor, Graduate School of Media and Governance, Keio University Research Fellow, Institute of Industrial Science, the University of Tokyo Directors Liaison for Financial Cryptography at MIT Media Lab.

  • Session 3 / Presentation 1 : Sensing: Application of Blockchain to Secure Distributed Storage of the Safecast Open Sensor Network Data (English) Pieter Franken Senior Researcher, Blockchain Laboratory, Keio Research Institute at SFC, Safecast, Representative Director   and Richard Roland Senior Student, Faculty of Environment and Information Studies, Keio University

  • Session 3 / Presentation 2 : Blockchain Technologies and Their Road towards Common Infrastructure (Japanese) Prof. Kanta Matsuura Professor, Institute of Industrial Science, the University of Tokyo

  • Session 3 / Presentation 3 : ScalingBitcoin 2018 (Japanese) Prof. Shin’Ichiro Matsuo (See Session 2 Presentation 2 for the affiliation)
