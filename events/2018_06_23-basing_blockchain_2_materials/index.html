Presentation material of 'The 2nd Workshop Basing Blockchain' now available

June 23, 2018

BASE Alliance and Keio University co-hosted a workshop on ongoing work on academic research on blockchain on June 23rd, 2018 at Mita Campus of Keio University.

The materials used at the workshop can be downloadble in the following links.

  • Opening Remarks (No Materials) Jun Murai Professor, Faculty of Environment and Information Studies, Keio University Dean, Graduate School of Media and Governance Director, Blockchain Laboratory, Keio Research Institute at SFC

  • Keynote (No Materials) Joi Ito Distinguished Researcher, Keio Research Institute at SFC / Director, MIT Media Lab.

  • Presentation 1 : BSafe.Network: International Academic Research Network - Status Update (English) Prof. Shin’Ichiro Matsuo Project Professor, Graduate School of Media and Governance, Keio University Research Fellow, Institute of Industrial Science, the University of Tokyo Directors Liaison for Financial Cryptography at MIT Media Lab.

  • Presentation 2 : Explicit Re-formulation of POW-type Blockchain Security Proof and Its Benefits (Japanese) Takuro Hosoi Technical Specialist, Department of Informatics and Electronics, IIS, The University of Tokyo

  • Presentation 3 : Layer 2 solution for cross-chain with payment channel (Japanese) Hiroki WATANABE NTT Service Evolution Laboratories
